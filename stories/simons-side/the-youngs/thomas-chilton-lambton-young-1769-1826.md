```
Rev. Thomas Chilton Lambton Young of Emmanuel College, Cam- 
bridge, B.A. 1791, M.A. 1794, Domestic Chaplain to James, 5th 
Earl of Cardigan; Rector of Cranoe, co. Leicester, 1808, Rector of 
Dodbrooke, co. Devon, 1817 (son of Robert Young of Chiswick by 
Dorothy his wife, dau. and heiress of Robert Chilton of Little Chilton 
in the parish of Houghton le Spring, co. Durham) ; born at Turn- 
ham Green 5 October (according to entry in the family Bible), 
bapt. at Chiswick 16 October 1769 ; died at Dodbrooke 21 August, 
bur. in the Chancel of Dodbrooke Church 26 August 1826. M.L
```
