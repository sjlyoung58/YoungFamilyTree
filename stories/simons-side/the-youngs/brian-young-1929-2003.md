## Dad's secound cousin, hence my second cousin once removed ([tree](http://sjly.co.uk/webtrees/hourglass.php?rootid=I783&ged=SYJY))

Brian was a serious genealogist, doing a lot of research in the 1980s before we had the benefit of computers and sites like Ancestry to make the whole exercise so much easier.

Brian visited my Grandparents several times during his research, and also Mum and Dad. I have a number of informative letters he wrote to them that have helped my own research a lot.
