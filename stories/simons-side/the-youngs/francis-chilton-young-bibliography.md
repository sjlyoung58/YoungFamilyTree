# Francis Chilton Young - Bibliography

*Extracted from Confluence FamilyTree wiki (exported 2 November 2012)*

---

FCY was author of a number of books and editor (or part of the editorial team) on a number of others.

It seems he was something of a D.I.Y. pioneer judging from the subject matter of some of his books, others (earlier works) are mainly school and reference books.
