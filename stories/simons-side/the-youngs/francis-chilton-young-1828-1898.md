## My Great (x2) Grandfather ([tree](http://sjly.co.uk/webtrees/hourglass.php?show_full=1&rootid=I81&generations=3&box_width=100))

FCY was an [author of many books](https://www.google.co.uk/search?tbo=p&tbm=bks&q=inauthor:%22Francis+Chilton+Young%22), and worked with Beeton's (amongst others) on several other works including [Beeton's dictionary of universal information](http://www.amazon.com/Beetons-dictionary-universal-information-pronunciation/dp/B00086RY8M) of which I have a copy (4 volumes) - FCY is named on most of the fold out maps. I have created a bibliography of his work on another page.

There seems to be a whiff of both scandal and financial problems around FCY with an allegedly bigamous second marriage (although FCY was not aware his second wife was abandoned, not widowed), and money problems (he was declared bankrupt when the private school he was running failed) despite some significant 'windfalls' and apparently good employment over the years. Towards the end of his life he applied for pension from the First Lord of the Treasury.
