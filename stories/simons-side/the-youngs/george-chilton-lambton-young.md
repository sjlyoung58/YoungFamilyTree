Hi, I am new to this list so I will throw my genealogical hat in the ring.  
John CARTER eloped with Dorothy CHILTON and they were married in St.  
George's Chapel, London 3 Oct 1750. They were from Chester-le-Street,  
Durham. She was a blue blood from her LAMBTON mother and he was a footman so  
it must have been a family scandal ! Anyway, they had 2 children and then  
John CARTER died. His widow Dorothy (CHILTON) CARTER remarried to Robert  
YOUNG 1757, and they seemed to have lived out their days in Middlesex Co.  
Dorothy was buried 1806 in Iver Church Cem. where their son George Chilton  
Lambton YOUNG was a minister. They had children Thomas, Anne, Catherine,  
Dorothy, Robert, George and the boys always had Lambton Chilton for their  
middle names. Now from Dorothy's first marriage to John CARTER was a son  
John Chilton Lambton CARTER b. 1752 where? and he is the object of over 25  
year's search. I presume he was raised with the YOUNGs so I am looking for  
any reference to his name. Perhaps someone researching the YOUNG surname has  
seen his name?????  
Many thanks for your time,  
Suzanne Lisson
