# Letter to Victor on Family Tree (Collins)

*Extracted from Confluence FamilyTree wiki (exported 2 November 2012)*

---

This page contains a letter from "cousin Frances" to Victor which appears to explain the Collins family lineage mainly (Victor's paternal grandmother)

Firstly below is the original letter, and then what appears to be Victor's notes based on this letter.

## Original Letter

This letter was written across both sides of several small pieces of paper 11 x 7 cm. I presume these were originally joined together like Victor's notes below, but have become separated by age. This makes it quite a challenge to piece together the 12 parts in the correct order.
