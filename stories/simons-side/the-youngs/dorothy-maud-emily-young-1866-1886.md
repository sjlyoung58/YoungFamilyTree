### Confusion

I think it is most likely that 'our' Dorothy M E Young did die in 1886, and the Dorothy Young in the Gracewood tree is a different person mistakenly linked?

Auntie Gertie's memoirs state the following -  
"Of our father's beginnings we know little, except that he too was one of nine children and that he was born in Newton Abbott, Devon. They were a very delicate and unfortunate family. Their father was a strange and impecunious man, who had money at times from rich relations, but soon spent it. He was a "literary" man and had some connection with Mrs. Beeton and her publishers, and for this reason he came from Devon to Greenhithe, where I understand Mrs. Beeton was living. **Five of his children died from tuberculosis at an early age, one lived to 35, and of the three remaining sons - one died in Australia in middle age, while the twins, my father and Uncle Julian - lived to the ages of 90 and 82 respectively**."
