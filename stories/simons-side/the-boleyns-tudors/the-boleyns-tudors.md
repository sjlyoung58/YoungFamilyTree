### King Henry VIII

If [Catherine Carey](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catherine_Carey) was the illegitimate child of [Henry VIII](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_VIII_of_England) and Mary Boleyn [as thought by some historians](http://www.theanneboleynfiles.com/965/did-henry-viii-father-mary-careys-children/), then King Henry VIII was my Great (x14) Grandfather

This PDF is recently discovered information making it extremely likely that Catherine Carey was the elder of Mary Boleyn's two children (previously a matter of some dispute) which increases the likelihood of Henry VIII being her father.
