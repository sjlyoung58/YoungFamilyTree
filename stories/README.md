### Major Narratives

These are the richest documents — extended personal narratives and family histories:

1. **[Auntie Gertie's Memoirs](simons-side/the-youngs/auntie-gerties-memoirs.md)** — Gertrude Young's full autobiography. Growing up in Gravesend in the early 1900s, Victor Young & Son estate agency, WWI, family life. Extraordinarily vivid personal writing.

2. **[Chronicles of the Mullord Family](julies-side/the-mullords/chronicles-of-the-mullord-family.md)** — Comprehensive history of the Mullord family with extensive census records, family stories, and genealogical research across multiple generations.

3. **[Francis Chilton Young — Bibliography](simons-side/the-youngs/francis-chilton-young-bibliography.md)** — Complete bibliography of FCY's published works: poetry, novels, periodical contributions. Full tables of contents.

4. **[Victor Young & Son](simons-side/the-youngs/victor-young-son.md)** — History of the Gravesend estate agency founded by Victor Edward Lambton Young.

5. **[The Schooner, The Jane Slade](simons-side/the-slades/the-schooner-the-jane-slade.md)** — History of the schooner built at Polruan 1862–63. The ship was named after Jane Slade (née Salt), who had a figurehead carved in her likeness.

6. **[Donald Young's notes on the family home](simons-side/the-youngs/donald-youngs-notes-on-the-family-home.md)** — Transcription of handwritten notes about 23 Harmer Street, Gravesend. Scanned manuscript pages included.

7. **[Dorothy Maud Emily Young 1866–1886](simons-side/the-youngs/dorothy-maud-emily-young-1866-1886.md)** — Inquest and death certificate research into Dorothy's death at age 20.

8. **[Memo to the First Lord of the Treasury](simons-side/the-youngs/memo-to-the-first-lord-of-the-treasury.md)** — 1875 memo by Francis Chilton Young on Metropolitan & District Railway costs.
